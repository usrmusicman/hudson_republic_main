## Definitions
All capitalized terms used in this Code shall be interpreted in accordance with their definitions in the referenced instruments below.

- **Sedition**: The intentional incitement of rebellion or insurrection against the constitutional order of the Hudson Republic with the specific aim of overthrowing or materially undermining legitimate government authority.
- **Material Support**: The provision of funds, resources, weapons, intelligence, shelter, or other tangible assistance that directly aids sedition.
- **Sequential Mandate**: Cumulative sentencing for repeated or escalating violations, served consecutively, as defined in **[Criminal Categorization Act](../CA/CA_CRIMECATEGORIZATION_20260401.md)**.

---

## Preamble
This Legal Code defines the offense of sedition, its elements, classification, and penalties. It operates as a subordinate instrument to **[Criminal Categorization Act](../CA/CA_CRIMECATEGORIZATION_20260401.md)** and protects the constitutional stability of the Republic while safeguarding the immutable rights of the Individual under **[The Individual (Sovereign) Act](../CA/CA_THEINDIVIDUAL_20260401.md)**. All proceedings shall adhere to strict due process and proportionality.

---

## Purpose
The purpose of this Code is to punish deliberate incitement against the constitutional order while maintaining narrow, objective criteria that prevent abuse or overreach. It supplies prosecutors and courts with precise standards for charging and sentencing.

---

## Provisions

### Not Considered Criminal Activity
Lawful assembly, speech, expression, protest, or rallying protected under **[The Individual (Sovereign) Act](../CA/CA_THEINDIVIDUAL_20260401.md)** does not constitute sedition.

### Criminal Priority Level
Sedition is a minimum **Level 6 (Severe Felony)** and may escalate to a maximum **Level 8 (Capital High Crime)**.

### Charge Criteria (All elements must be proven)
1. The accused deliberately incited rebellion or insurrection against the constitutional order.  
2. The accused acted with specific intent to overthrow or materially undermine legitimate government authority.

### Charge Upgrade (All additional elements must be proven)
The offense upgrades if the accused provided **material support** to individuals or organizations actively engaged in incitement, insurrection, or related violence.

---

## Sentencing
- **Base Sentence**: Minimum 15 years imprisonment (first offense).  
- **Second Offense**: Minimum 30 years imprisonment.  
- **Third Offense**: Mandatory natural life imprisonment (Level 8).  

All sentences are subject to the **Sequential Mandate**.

### Restitution
A mandatory restitution fee of **ten (10) troy ounces of gold** shall be imposed.  
If the convicted individual cannot pay, they must enter a **prison labour agreement** at the rate of **one-eighth (1/8) troy ounce of silver per hour** or face the maximum allowable sentence.

### Appeals
The accused is entitled to a maximum of **three appeals** per **[Criminal Appeals Act](../LA/LA_CRIMINALAPPEALS_20260401.md)**.

---

## Compliance and Non-Compliance
Failure to comply with restitution or prison labour constitutes a separate offense and may result in additional charges under this Code or **[Criminal Categorization Act](../CA/CA_CRIMECATEGORIZATION_20260401.md)**. Willful non-compliance triggers immediate enforcement, including asset forfeiture.

---

## Conflicts
This Code may overlap with **[Treason (Criminal) Code](./CO_TREASON_20260401.md)**. Prosecutors shall charge the most serious applicable offense, with sentencing governed by the higher classification and the Sequential Mandate.
