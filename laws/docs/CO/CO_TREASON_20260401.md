## Definitions
All capitalized terms used in this Code shall be interpreted in accordance with their definitions in the referenced instruments below.

- **Treason**: The willful, knowing betrayal of the Hudson Republic by providing material support to declared enemies, terrorist networks, or organized crime syndicates with the intent to deprive the state or its citizens of fundamental rights.
- **Material Support**: The provision of funds, resources, weapons, intelligence, shelter, or other tangible assistance that directly aids treasonous acts.
- **Sequential Mandate**: Cumulative sentencing for repeated or escalating violations, served consecutively, as defined in **[Criminal Categorization Act](../CA/CA_CRIMECATEGORIZATION_20260401.md)**.

---

## Preamble
This Legal Code defines the offense of treason, its elements, classification, and penalties. As the gravest crime against the Republic, treason is subject to the highest scrutiny. This Code operates as a subordinate instrument to **[Criminal Categorization Act](../CA/CA_CRIMECATEGORIZATION_20260401.md)** and protects the sovereignty and constitutional order of the Republic while preserving the immutable rights of the Individual under **[The Individual (Sovereign) Act](../CA/CA_THEINDIVIDUAL_20260401.md)**. All proceedings shall adhere to strict due process and proportionality.

---

## Purpose
The purpose of this Code is to punish the ultimate betrayal of the Republic with narrow, objective criteria that prevent abuse or overreach while providing prosecutors and courts with precise standards for charging and sentencing.

## Provisions

### Not Considered Criminal Activity
Lawful assembly, speech, expression, protest, or rallying protected under **[The Individual (Sovereign) Act](../CA/CA_THEINDIVIDUAL_20260401.md)** does not constitute treason.

### Criminal Priority Level
Treason is a minimum **Level 8 (Capital High Crime)** and may escalate to a maximum **Level 9 (Absolute Conviction)**.

### Charge Criteria (All elements must be proven)
1. The accused willingly, knowingly, and with intent provided material support to a known terrorist network, organized crime syndicate, or declared enemy of the Republic (as designated by the Hudson Intelligence Service or Interpol).  
2. The accused benefited materially (financially, politically, or personally) from the act.  
3. The action resulted in the deprivation of rights guaranteed under **[The Individual (Sovereign) Act](../CA/CA_THEINDIVIDUAL_20260401.md)**.

### Charge Upgrade (All additional elements must be proven)
The offense upgrades to Level 9 if the treasonous act directly or indirectly caused the deprivation of life(s) and the accused acted with willful intent to harm individuals.

---

## Sentencing
- **Base Sentence**: Mandatory natural life imprisonment (Level 8).  
- **Upgraded Sentence**: Death penalty (Level 9).  

All sentences are subject to the **Sequential Mandate**.

### Death Penalty Safeguards
1. Automatic review by the Supreme Court.  
2. The National Representative must personally review the case and may exercise clemency or veto.  
3. Mandatory “Call of Mercy” hearing before the Riding Representative.  
4. Execution may not proceed if any procedural error, new exculpatory evidence, or constitutional violation is identified.

### Restitution
A mandatory restitution fee of **thirty (30) troy ounces of gold** shall be imposed.  
If the convicted individual cannot pay, they must enter a **prison labour agreement** at the rate of **one-eighth (1/8) troy ounce of silver per hour** or face reduced amenities during their sentence.

### Appeals
The accused is entitled to a maximum of **three appeals** per **[Criminal Appeals Act](../LA/LA_CRIMINALAPPEALS_20260401.md)**.

---

## Compliance and Non-Compliance
Failure to comply with restitution or prison labour constitutes a separate offense and may result in additional charges under this Code or **[Criminal Categorization Act](../CA/CA_CRIMECATEGORIZATION_20260401.md)**. Willful non-compliance triggers immediate enforcement, including asset forfeiture.

---

## Conflicts
This Code may overlap with **[Sedition (Criminal) Code](./CO_SEDITION_20260401.md)**. Prosecutors shall charge the most serious applicable offense, with sentencing governed by the higher classification and the Sequential Mandate.
