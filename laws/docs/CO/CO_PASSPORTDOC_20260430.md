## Definitions
- **Hudson Passport**: The official travel and identity document issued exclusively to citizens of the Hudson Republic.  
- **Biometric Data**: Facial image and fingerprints stored in a secure electronic chip compliant with international standards.  
- **Kinetic Holographic Beaver**: A multi-angle parallax security hologram on the front cover of the Hudson Passport featuring a beaver in three distinct colored positions visible through a sweeping parallax effect.  
- **Felony**: Any criminal conviction under Hudson Republic law that results in permanent disqualification from holding a Hudson Passport.

---

## Preamble
This Legislative Code establishes the mandatory technical specifications, security features, eligibility criteria, and issuance standards for the Hudson Republic Passport. The passport serves as proof of citizenship while incorporating advanced anti-counterfeiting technology that prominently showcases the beaver as the enduring symbol of the Republic.

---

## Purpose
The purpose of this Code is to define the precise contents, design specifications, and security features required for a valid Hudson Passport. It ensures high resistance to forgery and global interoperability while featuring a distinctive kinetic holographic element that reinforces national identity.

---

## Provisions

**Section 1 — Eligibility**  
1.1 Hudson Passports shall be issued **exclusively** to naturalized or birth citizens of the Hudson Republic who have no felony convictions under Hudson Republic law.  
1.2 Dual citizenship is strictly prohibited. Applicants must renounce any other nationality prior to issuance.  
1.3 Issuance authority rests solely with the Hudson Passport Services Authority pursuant to the **[Immigration and Citizenship Act](../LA/LA_IMMIGRATION_20260401.md)**.

**Section 2 — Required Contents and Security Features**  
A valid Hudson Passport must include the following:

- **Cover Design**: Durable, high-quality material prominently displaying the words “Hudson Republic Passport” and the official emblem of the Republic.  

- **Kinetic Holographic Beaver**: The front cover shall feature an advanced multi-angle parallax holographic security element depicting a beaver in three distinct colored positions. This hologram creates a sweeping parallax illusion: as the viewer’s eye moves horizontally across the cover from left to right (approximately 0° to 180° viewing angle), the beaver smoothly transitions between the three positions. The three positions are as follows:

  1. **0° (Leftmost position)**: A yellow beaver with its head and body pointing leftward horizontally.  
  2. **90° (Center position)**: A blue beaver standing tall vertically with its head pointing leftward.  
  3. **180° (Rightmost position)**: A green beaver with its head and body pointing rightward horizontally.  

  This colored parallax effect provides both a striking visual identifier and a robust anti-counterfeiting feature.

- **Biographical Data Page**: Full legal name, date and place of birth, nationality (“Citizen of the Hudson Republic”), passport number, date of issue, date of expiry, place of issue, and holder’s signature.  
- **Facial Photograph**: High-resolution biometric photograph meeting ICAO Doc 9303 standards (neutral expression, full bare face, plain light background).  
- **Biometric Chip**: Secure embedded electronic chip containing the facial image and two flat fingerprints in internationally interoperable formats.  
- **Machine Readable Zone (MRZ)**: Compliant with ICAO Doc 9303 standards.  
- **Additional Security Elements**: Guilloche patterns, microprinting, color-shifting inks, UV-reactive features, and other overt and covert security measures as determined by the Hudson Passport Services Authority.

**Section 3 — Fees**  
All application, issuance, renewal, and replacement fees shall be paid exclusively in physical bullion pursuant to the **[Legal Tender Act](../CA/CA_LEGALTENDER_20260401.md)** and detailed in the **[Immigration Application Schedule](../SCH/SCH_IMMIGRATIONAPP_20260413.md)**.

## Procedures
Applications for Hudson Passports shall be processed in accordance with procedures and fee schedules outlined in the **[Immigration Application Schedule](../SCH/SCH_IMMIGRATIONAPP_20260413.md)**. Quality assurance verification shall include confirmation that the kinetic holographic beaver correctly displays all three colored positions under the specified sweeping parallax effect.

---

## Compliance
Any forgery, alteration, or fraudulent use of a Hudson Passport constitutes a serious offense under Hudson Republic law. The Hudson Passport Services Authority reserves the right to revoke a passport for cause, including subsequent felony convictions or violations of citizenship rules. Unauthorized possession or use by non-citizens is prohibited.

---

## Conflicts
This Code shall be interpreted in harmony with the **[Immigration and Citizenship Act](../LA/LA_IMMIGRATION_20260401.md)** and the **[Individual (Sovereign) Act](../CA/CA_THEINDIVIDUAL_20260401.md)**. In the event of any conflict, priority shall be given to the principles of individual sovereignty and strict citizenship requirements.
