# National Security Act

---

## Definitions
All capitalized terms used in this Act shall be interpreted in accordance with their definitions in the referenced instruments below.

- **Hudson Intelligence Service (HIS)**: The federal intelligence and policing agency of the Hudson Republic.  
- **Hudson Border Control (HBC)**: Specialized federal police task force responsible for border security and immigration enforcement.  
- **Hudson Port Authority (HPA)**: Federal agency responsible for port security, cargo inspection, and tariff collection.  
- **Operations General**: Senior operational commander with authority over multi-jurisdictional security actions.  
- **Sequential Mandate**: Cumulative sentencing for violations of fiduciary duty, treason, or sedition.  
- **The Individual (Sovereign) Act**: Refers to **[The Individual (Sovereign) Act](./CA_THEINDIVIDUAL_20260401.md)**.

---

## Preamble
The national security of the Hudson Republic is a core federal responsibility exercised solely to protect the sovereignty of the Republic, the immutable rights of the Individual, and the territorial integrity of its Ridings. This Act establishes the **Hudson Intelligence Service (HIS)**, **Hudson Border Control (HBC)**, and **Hudson Port Authority (HPA)** as coordinated federal agencies. Each agency maintains clearly defined departments and operates under strict constitutional limits. All operations, investigations, and clearances shall be conducted with transparency, accountability, and full respect for the rights enumerated in **[The Individual (Sovereign) Act](./CA_THEINDIVIDUAL_20260401.md)**. All fees, fines, and financial obligations under this Act shall be paid exclusively in physical bullion pursuant to the **[Legal Tender Act](./CA_LEGALTENDER_20260401.md)**.

## Section 1 — Hudson Intelligence Service (HIS)
**1.1** The HIS is the federal intelligence and policing agency of the Republic.  

**1.2 Responsibilities**:  
* Intelligence gathering and analysis strictly bounded by the Constitution.  
* National and international policing coordination.  
* Conducting all security clearances and background checks.  

**1.3 Departments**:  
* Gangs and Terrorism Unit: Domestic office-based investigations.  
* International Affairs Unit: Global criminal and terrorism rings.  
* Forensics & Investigative Lab Units: On-site and laboratory forensics.  
* Financial Crimes Unit: Counterfeit, fraud, and embezzlement.  
* Drug Trafficking Unit: Joint operations with the HPA.  
* General Patrol Force: Specialized federal patrolling units.  
* Airport Unit: Heavily armed airport security.

---

## Section 2 — Hudson Border Control (HBC)
**2.1** The HBC is a specialized federal police task force and sister agency to **Hudson Passport Services**.  

**2.2 Responsibilities**:  
* First point of contact for all foreigners at the border.  
* Execution of deportations per the Immigration and Citizenship Act.  
* Detention authority and foreign national record-keeping.  

**2.3 Departments**:  
* Human Trafficking Unit: Office-based investigations.  
* Patrol Force: Armed border patrol.  
* Special Operations Unit: SWAT and crowd-control.  
* DEEP Unit (Deportation, Excavation, Evacuation, and Patrol): Specialized in locating overstayers, illegal immigrants, and rescue operations.

---

## Section 3 — Hudson Port Authority (HPA)
**3.1** The HPA is responsible for the security and efficiency of all ports and border crossings.  

**3.2 Responsibilities**:  
* Cargo inspection, tariff collection, and fine reconciliation.  
* Seizure of prohibited items per the Borders and Duties Act.  

**3.3 Departments**:  
* Logistics and Operations Unit: Floor protocols and storage.  
* Security Unit: Heavily armed on-site guards.  
* Processing Office: Paperwork, tariff tabulation, and claim reconciliation.  
* Audit Team: Uses XRF technology to assess bullion payments and verify cargo declarations.

---

## Section 4 — Operations General
**4.1** Responsible for the legal transfer of information and resources during multi-jurisdictional cases.  
**4.2 Authorization**: Requires the **written signature of the National Representative** for every action.  
**4.3 Clearance**: Must hold **Top (Top Secret) Clearance**. Only one Operations General per site or field team.

---

## Section 5 — Inter-Agency Collaboration
**5.1 National Security Declaration**: The National Representative may mandate immediate inter-agency collaboration via written declaration.  
**5.2 Compliance**: All collaboration must remain in full compliance with **[The Individual (Sovereign) Act](./CA_THEINDIVIDUAL_20260401.md)**.

---

## Section 6 — Security Clearances
Security clearances are issued by the HIS per the **[General Administration Act](./CA_GENERALADMIN_20260401.md)**.

**6.1 Normal Clearance**: Standard criminal background check (vulnerable sector check required for specific roles).  
**6.2 Enhanced Clearance**: Background check plus a **video-recorded polygraph examination** before an investigative panel.  
**6.3 Top (Top Secret) Clearance**: All Enhanced requirements plus evaluation by the **Hudson United Guard (HUG)**. Requires a certificate signed by the National Representative.

---

## Section 7 — General Provisions
**7.1 Reinvestigation**: Clearances are subject to periodic review and immediate revocation for breach of oath.  
**7.2 Penalties**: Unauthorized disclosure of information triggers the **Sequential Mandate** and prosecution for breach of fiduciary duty.
