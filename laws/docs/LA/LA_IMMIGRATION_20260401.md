# Immigration and Citizenship Act

---

## Definitions
All capitalized terms used in this Act shall be interpreted in accordance with their definitions in the referenced instruments below.

- **Guest**: Temporary visitor status with limited stay rights.  
- **Resident**: Holder of an Authorized Resident Certificate (ARC).  
- **Naturalized Citizen**: Person granted full citizenship through the Pathway to Citizenship.  
- **Pathway to Citizenship (PTC)**: The formal process for Residents to become citizens.  
- **Authorized Resident Certificate (ARC)**: 10-year renewable residency permit.  
- **FARM Visa**: Foreign Agricultural and Resource Management temporary work visa.

---

## Preamble
The Hudson Republic maintains a controlled, merit-based immigration system that prioritizes national security, economic sustainability, cultural cohesion, and the primacy of citizen sovereignty. Immigration serves the Republic, not the reverse. All fees, fines, restitution, licensing, and financial settlements under this Act shall be paid exclusively in physical bullion pursuant to the **[Legal Tender Act](../CA/CA_LEGALTENDER_20260401.md)**. Non-citizens (Guests and Residents) are prohibited from owning land or accessing government-funded social assistance or welfare; all services must be paid at fair market value in bullion. Every entrant—alien or citizen—must hold a valid internationally recognized passport prior to entry.

## Section 1 — Legal Alien Statuses
The Republic recognizes three non-citizen statuses:  
1. **Guest**  
2. **Resident** (Authorized Resident Certificate holder)  
3. **Naturalized Citizen**

---

## Section 2 — Guest Status
**2.1 Entry**: Guests may enter visa-free for up to thirty (30) days.  
**2.2 Guest Visa**: Stays exceeding 30 days require a visa costing **one (1) troy ounce of silver**.  
**2.3 Renewal & Limits**:  
* Must be renewed every three (3) months for 1 troy oz of silver.  
* Maximum stay is **two (2) years** from initial entry unless granted ARC status.  
* Failure to depart triggers immediate deportation and overstay penalties.  

**2.4 Re-Entry Restrictions**: Any non-citizen departing after the two-year mark is subject to a mandatory **one-year re-entry bar**.  
**2.5 Employment**: Domestic employment is prohibited; remote or digital nomad work for foreign entities is permitted.  
**2.6 Automatic Bar**: Entry is barred for those with felony convictions, active warrants, security flags, or outstanding foreign debts.  
**2.7 Overstay Penalties**: Fines are **one (1) troy ounce of silver per day**. Results in deportation and a five-year re-entry ban.

---

## Section 3 — Resident Status (Authorized Resident Certificate – ARC)
**3.1 Issuance**: Issued by **Hudson Passport Services**.  
**3.2 Validity**: 10 years, with compliance audits every 2 years.  
**3.3 Employment**: Permitted for domestic employers.  
**3.4 Compliance**: Any felony, misdemeanor, or security threat triggers immediate revocation and deportation.  
**3.5 Restrictions**: No government-funded assistance; land ownership is **prohibited**.  
**3.6 Application Fee**: **One (1) troy ounce of gold**.  
**3.7 Quota**: Annual limits are determined by a 60% clear majority vote in the House of Constituents.

---

## Section 4 — Naturalized Citizens
**4.1 Origin**: Granted via the **Pathway to Citizenship (PTC)**.  
**4.2 Rights**: Full constitutional protections under **[The Individual (Sovereign) Act](../CA/CA_THEINDIVIDUAL_20260401.md)**.  
**4.3 Land Ownership**: Permitted (exclusive to citizens).

---

## Section 5 — Pathway to Citizenship (PTC)
**5.1 Screening**: In ARC year 9, two interviews with an Integration Specialist are required (4 months apart).  
**5.2 Fees**: **One (1) troy ounce of gold** per interview; **one (1) troy ounce of gold** for the PTC Examination.  
**5.3 Examination**: Minimum 80% score required; taken in the final 4 months of ARC validity.  
**5.4 Failure**: Departure required upon ARC expiry; no extensions granted.

---

## Section 6 — Passport Services
**6.1 Eligibility**: Passports are issued **only** to citizens.  
**6.2 Fees**:  
* 5-year: **One (1) troy ounce of silver**.  
* 10-year: **Two (2) troy ounces of silver**.  
**6.3 Photo Specs**: Full bare face required per the Passport Code.

---

## Section 7 — Hudson Passport Services Authority
**7.1** Exclusive authority to issue visas, ARCs, PTC documents, citizenship certificates, and passports. No third-party issuance permitted.

---

## Section 8 — Renunciation and Re-Application
**8.1** Renouncing citizens must possess citizenship of another state. Dual citizenship is strictly prohibited.  
**8.2** Former citizens wishing to rejoin must apply for an ARC and follow all standard procedures.

---

## Section 9 — General Provisions
**9.1 Bullion Mandate**: All fees and fines paid exclusively in physical bullion per the **[Legal Tender Act](../CA/CA_LEGALTENDER_20260401.md)**.  
**9.2 Amendment**: This Act may only be amended via Constitutional Article procedure.

---

## Section 10 — Foreign Agricultural and Resource Management (FARM) Visa
**10.1 Purpose**: Temporary work visa for seasonal agricultural and resource sectors.  
**10.2 Rounding-Down Rule**: All calculations for quotas or positions must be rounded down to the nearest whole number.  
**10.3 Business Penalties**: Sponsoring businesses failing to comply face a penalty of **one (1) troy ounce of gold per count**.  
**10.4 Local Hiring Requirements**:  
* Businesses must declare the exact number of full-time seasonal positions.  
* **At least 50%** of full-time positions must be filled by Hudson Citizens or ARC holders.  
* **Part-time positions** are reserved **exclusively** for Hudson Citizens; no FARM visa holders permitted.
