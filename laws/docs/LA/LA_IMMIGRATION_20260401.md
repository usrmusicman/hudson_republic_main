## Definitions
All capitalized terms used in this Act shall be interpreted in accordance with their definitions in the referenced instruments below.

- **Guest**: Temporary visitor status with limited stay rights.  
- **Resident**: Holder of an Authorized Resident Certificate (ARC).  
- **Naturalized Citizen**: Person granted full citizenship through the Pathway to Citizenship (PTC).  
- **Pathway to Citizenship (PTC)**: The formal process for Residents to become citizens.  
- **Authorized Resident Certificate (ARC)**: 10-year renewable residency permit.  
- **FARM Visa**: Foreign Agricultural and Resource Management temporary work visa.

## Preamble
The Hudson Republic maintains a controlled, merit-based immigration system that prioritizes national security, economic sustainability, cultural cohesion, and the primacy of citizen sovereignty. Immigration serves the Republic, not the reverse. Non-citizens (Guests and Residents) are prohibited from owning land or accessing government-funded social assistance or welfare; all services must be paid at fair market value in bullion. Every entrant—alien or citizen—must hold a valid internationally recognized passport prior to entry. All fees, fines, restitution, licensing, and financial settlements under this Act shall be paid exclusively in physical bullion pursuant to the **[Legal Tender Act](../CA/CA_LEGALTENDER_20260401.md)** and are detailed in the **[Immigration Application Schedule](../SCH/SCH_IMMIGRATIONAPP_20260413.md)**.

## Section 1 — Legal Alien Statuses
The Republic recognizes three non-citizen statuses:  
1. **Guest**  
2. **Resident** (Authorized Resident Certificate holder)  
3. **Naturalized Citizen**

All specific fees, conditions, renewal rules, and administrative requirements for these statuses are set out in the **[Immigration Application Schedule](../SCH/SCH_IMMIGRATIONAPP_20260413.md)**.

## Section 4 — Naturalized Citizens
**4.1 Origin**: Granted via the **Pathway to Citizenship (PTC)**.  
**4.2 Rights**: Full constitutional protections under **[The Individual (Sovereign) Act](../CA/CA_THEINDIVIDUAL_20260401.md)**.  
**4.3 Land Ownership**: Permitted (exclusive to citizens).

All PTC screening, fees, examination requirements, and timelines are set out in the **[Immigration Application Schedule](../SCH/SCH_IMMIGRATIONAPP_20260413.md)**.

---

## Section 5 — Passport Services
**5.1 Eligibility**: Passports are issued **only** to citizens.  
**5.2 Fees**: See the **[Immigration Application Schedule](../SCH/SCH_IMMIGRATIONAPP_20260413.md)**.  
**5.3 Photo Specs**: Full bare face required per the Passport Code.

---

## Section 6 — Hudson Passport Services Authority
**6.1** Exclusive authority to issue visas, ARCs, PTC documents, citizenship certificates, and passports. No third-party issuance permitted.

---

## Section 7 — Renunciation and Re-Application
**7.1** Renouncing citizens must possess citizenship of another state. Dual citizenship is strictly prohibited.  
**7.2** Former citizens wishing to rejoin must apply for an ARC and follow all standard procedures set out in the **[Immigration Application Schedule](../SCH/SCH_IMMIGRATIONAPP_20260413.md)**.

---

## Section 8 — General Provisions
**8.1 Bullion Mandate**: All fees and fines shall be paid exclusively in physical bullion per the **[Legal Tender Act](../CA/CA_LEGALTENDER_20260401.md)**.  
**8.2 Amendment**: This Act may only be amended via Constitutional Article procedure.

---

## Section 9 — Foreign Agricultural and Resource Management (FARM) Visa
**9.1 Purpose**: Temporary work visa for seasonal agricultural and resource sectors.  
**9.2 Rounding-Down Rule**: All calculations for quotas or positions must be rounded down to the nearest whole number.  
**9.3 Local Hiring Requirements**:  
* Businesses must declare the exact number of full-time seasonal positions.  
* **At least 50%** of full-time positions must be filled by Hudson Citizens or ARC holders.  
* **Part-time positions** are reserved **exclusively** for Hudson Citizens; no FARM visa holders permitted.  

All FARM-specific fees, penalties, and compliance rules are set out in the **[Immigration Application Schedule](../SCH/SCH_IMMIGRATIONAPP_20260413.md)**.
