# Immigration Application Schedule

---

## Definitions

- **Guest**: Temporary visitor status with limited stay rights.  
- **Resident**: Holder of an Authorized Resident Certificate (ARC).  
- **Naturalized Citizen**: Person granted full citizenship through the Pathway to Citizenship (PTC).  
- **Pathway to Citizenship (PTC)**: The formal multi-step process for Residents to become full citizens.  
- **Authorized Resident Certificate (ARC)**: 10-year renewable residency permit.  
- **FARM Visa**: Foreign Agricultural and Resource Management temporary work visa.  
- **HCB8**: The smallest unit of Hudson Copper Bullion (1/8 troy ounce of 95% pure copper), used as the base unit for all fees in this schedule.  
- **Per Count**: Per violation or per day of violation, as applicable.

---

## Preamble

This schedule sets out the standardized fees, conditions, renewal rules, and administrative requirements for all immigration programs in the Hudson Republic. It enforces a controlled, merit-based system that prioritizes national security, economic sustainability, cultural cohesion, and citizen sovereignty. All fees, fines, and penalties are expressed in HCB8 (Hudson Copper Bullion units) and must be paid exclusively in physical bullion. This schedule is purely referential and derives its full authority from the Immigration and Citizenship Act (LA_IMMIGRATION_20260401).

---

## Usage

### Immigration Program Table
This table governs all non-citizen entry and stay programs.  
- **Program Type** determines the maximum stay, fees, and conditions.  
- **Issuance Date** and **Expiry Date** are recorded at the time of approval.  
- Fees are non-refundable and payable in physical bullion only.

### Pathway to Citizenship (PTC) Table
This table governs the final stage for Residents seeking full citizenship.  
- All PTC steps must be completed while holding a valid ARC.  
- Fees are non-refundable and payable in physical bullion only.
