## Definitions
- **Denomination**: The standardized weight of a bullion piece expressed in troy ounces.
- **Commercial Use**: Everyday transactions between merchants and individuals.
- **Institutional Use**: Banking, trusts, savings, and official reserves.

---

## Preamble
This Schedule provides the definitive list of acceptable bullion denominations and usage rules under the Legal Tender Act. It enables both standardized Hudson Republic minting and the Bring Your Own Bullion (BYOB) program by recognizing qualifying bullion from any source.
