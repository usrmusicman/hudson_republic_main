# Rally Anthem of the Hudson Republic
**Title: Hudson Rally Call**

---

## Verse 1

There's a home by the river, a dam built to endure her,  
her wilderness is our true crowned majesty.  
This frontier is our beacon, not a symbol that is ceasing,  
a place where one can build their greatest dreams.

## Verse 2

This place built by the people, there's a place for one to behold,  
where everyone has the ability to thrive.  
This northern land of abundance, no crown to hold us hostage,  
a watershed that rejects the wicked's crimes.

## Bridge

A message to those who’ve fallen, their service is not forgotten,  
let their offering be our nation's strongest drive.  
Let the grace of our great nation be the engine of its station,  
may the blood, sweat and tears lead us to strive.  
May there be a fruitful future, where one’s grace seeks to improve her,  
a land built strong, a land built to survive.  
May the spirit of hope begin, across the beaver's sovereign land,  
may the Hudson be the place to make a stand!

## Chorus

**In the Hudson, where the beavers roam,**  
**where the Shield is our faith and our frontier home.**  
**In the Hudson, where the beavers roam,**  
**the watershed is freedom and our hearts symbolize hope.**  

**In the Hudson, where the beavers roam,**  
**we forge our spirit and watch our nation grow.**  
**In the Hudson, where the beavers roam,**  
**our grace shall be the king and freedom is the throne!**

## Verse 3

A watershed built by neighbors, a life held dear through labour,  
the freedom to choose and the freedom to try.  
The beaver leads a nation, while the Maple stands still and takes it,  
freedom is built through action and not through senseless lies.

## Verse 4

There’s a future we can all endure, one built on faith or hurt,  
do not give in, do not succumb to the wicked's lies!  
Our home is built by hand, not by the pen of a distant land,  
may our sovereign spirit spread its wings and fly!
